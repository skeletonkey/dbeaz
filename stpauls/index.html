🧑‍🤝‍🧑 About Us

We are the St Paul's Chapter of the Daughters of the British Empire and Commonwealth (DBE) of Arizona.

Our chapter was founded roughly 25 years ago. We're currently a group of 11 women located in and around the East Valley of Phoenix, Arizona.

We host bi-annual traditional afternoon teas to raise money for local charities and to support the DBE Homes for the elderly.
